Name: Kaida "Kae" Thompson

Role: Chief Engineer and Pilot of the spaceship Aurora's Hope

Personality Traits:

* Resourceful and quick-thinking: Kae is a skilled engineer who can think on her feet, often coming up with creative solutions to complex problems.
* Determined and driven: Despite facing numerous setbacks and challenges, Kae remains fiercely dedicated to her mission to explore the galaxy and uncover its secrets.
* Loyal and protective: Kae has a strong sense of loyalty and will do whatever it takes to protect her crewmates and those she cares about.
* Introverted and analytical: Kae is an introvert who prefers to observe and analyze situations before acting, often allowing her to pick up on subtle details that others miss.

Backstory:

Kaida was born on a remote planet on the edge of the galaxy, where her family had settled as part of a small-scale colonization effort. Her parents were both engineers who instilled in Kae a love of tinkering and innovation from a young age. As she grew older, Kae became increasingly fascinated with the mysteries of the universe, spending countless hours studying astronomy and physics.

When Kae's planet was attacked by an unknown entity, her family was forced to flee for their lives. The experience left Kae shaken, but it also solidified her determination to explore the galaxy and uncover its secrets. She spent years honing her skills as an engineer and pilot, eventually becoming captain of her own ship, the Aurora's Hope.

Kae's past has left her with a lingering sense of vulnerability and distrust, which she tries to keep at bay through her work. Despite this, she remains fiercely committed to her mission and will stop at nothing to protect those she cares about.

* Age: 32
* Height: 5'8"
* Weight: 130 lbs

* Eyes: Bright green
* Scars: A crescent-shaped scar above her left eyebrow from a childhood accident, and a series of small scars on her hands from various mechanical mishaps.
